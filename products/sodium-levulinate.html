Sodium Levulinate

Mild, bio-based preservative and conditioning agent for clean, natural skin- and hair-care formulations.

Product overview

Sodium Levulinate is the sodium salt of levulinic acid, used as a natural-origin preservative and skin-conditioning agent in modern cosmetics and personal-care products.

Main Applications

  • Helps protect water-based formulations such as serums, gels, shampoos, and cleansers.
  • Supports skin softness, smoothness, and hydration.
  • Enhances hair feel and shine in shampoos, conditioners, and leave-on treatments.
  • Often used in blends with Sodium Anisate for stronger preservation systems.

Formulation Advantages

  • Plant-derived, vegan-friendly alternative in many "clean beauty" formulations.
  • Commonly effective in mildly acidic systems (often pH ~4‚Äì6).
  • Water-soluble and easy to add to the aqueous phase.
  • Works well in multi-component preservative blends.

Safety & Regulatory

  • INCI name: Sodium Levulinate.
  • Widely used in natural/organic-style formulation approaches.
  • Suitable for many sensitive-skin product types when used at recommended levels.
