Sodium Levulinate

Mild, bio-based preservative and skin-/hair-conditioning agent for clean and natural formulations.

Product overview

Sodium Levulinate (CAS No.: 539-88-8) is a water-soluble, white crystalline solid widely used in cosmetics and personal-care products. It functions as a mild preservative and conditioning agent, making it essential for modern clean-label and organic formulations.

Main Applications

  • Inhibits bacteria, yeasts, and molds in water-based products
  • Improves skin softness, smoothness, and hydration
  • Enhances hair softness and shine in shampoos and conditioners
  • Extends shelf life of serums, gels, and emulsions

  • Accepted under INCI nomenclature for global compliance
  • Approved for use in ECOCERT and COSMOS certified products
