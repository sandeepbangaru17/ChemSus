Sodium Levulinate

Sodium Levulinate (CAS No.: 539-88-8) is a water-soluble, white crystalline solid widely used in cosmetics and personal-care products as a mild preservative and skin-/hair-conditioning agent. Its easy incorporation into emulsions, shampoos, gels, serums, and creams makes it especially suitable for modern clean-label formulations.
