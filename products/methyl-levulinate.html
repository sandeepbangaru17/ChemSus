Product overview

Methyl Levulinate

Methyl Levulinate is a versatile ester derived from levulinic acid, widely used in biofuel formulations and as a building block for fine chemicals.

Applications

  • Biofuel blending and fuel additives
  • Performance solvents for coatings and cleaners
  • Chemical intermediates for specialty products

Features

  • High purity suitable for demanding applications
  • Renewable source from biomass-derived levulinic acid
  • Excellent solvency and formulation flexibility
