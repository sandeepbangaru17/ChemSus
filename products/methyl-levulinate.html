Product overview

Methyl Levulinate (CAS No.: 624-45-3) is a versatile bio-based ester derived from levulinic acid. It serves as a sustainable building block for specialty chemicals and a high-performance additive in biofuel formulations.

Key Applications

  • High-efficiency biofuel blending and fuel additives
  • Renewable performance solvent for coatings, inks, and cleaners
  • Versatile chemical intermediate for pharmaceutical and specialty synthesis
  • Precursor for bio-based resins and sustainable plasticizers

Core Features

  • High purity (>99%) for demanding industrial processes
  • 100% sourced from biomass-derived levulinic acid
  • Superior solvency and formulation flexibility
  • Biodegradable profile with low environmental toxicity
