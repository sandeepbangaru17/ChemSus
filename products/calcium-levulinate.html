Calcium Levulinate

Highly bioavailable calcium salt derived from biomass for pharmaceutical, nutraceutical, cosmetic, and agricultural applications.

Product Overview

CAS No.: 5743-49-7

Calcium Levulinate is a highly absorbable calcium salt of levulinic acid, offering excellent bioavailability and compatibility across multiple formulations.
