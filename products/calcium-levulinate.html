Calcium Levulinate

Premium biomass-derived mineral source for Pharmaceuticals, Nutraceuticals, and Agriculture.

Product overview

Chemical Identity

CAS No.: 5743-49-7

Calcium Levulinate is a highly absorbable calcium salt of levulinic acid, offering excellent bioavailability and compatibility across multiple formulations.

Medical & Health

Pharmaceutical & Veterinary Applications

  • Treatment of calcium deficiency and osteoporosis
  • Used in tablets, syrups, and injections
  • Veterinary use for milk fever and livestock health
  • Often combined with Vitamins D3 & B12
Wellness

Nutraceutical Applications

Used in dietary supplements and functional foods to support bone health, metabolism, and preventive nutrition.

Industry

Cosmetic & Agricultural Applications

  • Cosmetics: pH adjuster, skin conditioning, and formulation stabilizer for clean beauty.
  • Agriculture: Plant growth regulator and soil conditioner that improves nutrient uptake and crop yield.
  • Suitable for sustainable and organic farming systems.
