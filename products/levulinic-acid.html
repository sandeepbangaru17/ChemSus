Product overview

Its high reactivity combined with renewable origin makes it a cornerstone of green-chemistry and bio-based product development.

Agriculture & Herbicides

  • Used in the production of 5-aminolevulinic acid (ALA), a biodegradable herbicide and plant growth regulator
  • ALA is widely applied in agriculture, especially across Asian markets
  • Explored as an intermediate for sustainable crop-protection products
  • Supports eco-friendly and low-residue farming systems

Polymers, Plastics & Materials

  • Precursor for plasticizers, synthetic rubber, nylon, and resins
  • Improves flexibility and performance of materials such as PVC
  • Platform for derivatives like Œ≥-valerolactone and alkyl levulinates
  • Used in solvents, lubricants, and advanced polymer systems

Pharmaceuticals & Medical Uses

  • Intermediate in the synthesis of pharmaceuticals and fine chemicals
  • Related compounds (e.g., aminolevulinic acid) used in photodynamic therapy and diagnostic applications

Food, Flavors & Cosmetics

  • Used as a flavoring agent or intermediate in food and animal feed
  • Levulinic acid and its salts (e.g., sodium levulinate) used in personal care and cosmetics
  • Functions in preservation and skin-conditioning formulations
  • Permitted in many regions including the EU and the US

Other Industrial & Research Uses

  • Used in inks, coatings, antifreeze, textile dyes, and lubricants
  • Acts as a solvent or adsorbent in specialty formulations
  • Widely employed as a reagent and model compound in biomass-conversion and green-chemistry research
