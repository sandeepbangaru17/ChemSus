Bio-based solvent & fragrance ingredient

Ethyl Levulinate

Sustainable ester for flavours, fragrances, and advanced chemical synthesis.

Product overview

Ethyl Levulinate (CAS No.: 539-88-8) is a bio-based ester primarily used as a solvent, flavour and fragrance ingredient. Its low toxicity and renewable origin make it ideal for green chemistry and sustainable formulations.

Flavor & Fragrance

  • Food-grade flavouring for sweet, fruity notes in beverages and sweets
  • Used in personal-care products for its mild, creamy aroma
  • Supports fruity, floral, and gourmand fragrance accords

Solvent & Green Chemistry

  • Biodegradable solvent for coatings, paints, and cleaning agents
  • Effective in the extraction of natural botanical products
  • Renewable building block for levulinic-acid-derived specialty esters
