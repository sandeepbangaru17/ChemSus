Certificate of Recognition

ChemSus Technologies Private Limited is officially recognized as a startup by the Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT), Government of India, under the Startup India initiative.

The recognition acknowledges ChemSus' innovation in the specialty chemicals sector, with a focus on sustainable and green chemical processes using renewable lignocellulosic biomass.

This recognition strengthens ChemSus Technologies' credibility with investors, partners, and customers, and supports its mission to scale environmentally responsible chemical technologies.
