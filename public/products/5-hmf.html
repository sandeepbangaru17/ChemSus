Biomass-derived platform chemical

5-Hydroxymethylfurfural (5-HMF)

High-value intermediate from biomass enabling next-generation fuels, polymers, resins, and advanced research applications.

Product overview

5-Hydroxymethylfurfural (5-HMF, CAS No.: 67-47-0) is a key biomass-derived platform chemical used for producing fuels, polymers, resins, and a wide range of specialty and fine chemicals.

Chemical & Materials Uses

  • Precursor to 2,5-furandicarboxylic acid (FDCA) for producing PEF bioplastics
  • Conversion into bio-based solvents and fuel components such as 2,5-dimethylfuran (DMF)
  • Building block for fine chemicals, functional materials, and nanomaterials

Resins, Coatings & Construction

  • Alternative to formaldehyde in resins, adhesives, and coatings
  • Used in wood adhesives and foundry binders from bio-derived sources
  • Industrial-scale plants under development for construction and electronics uses

Biofuels & Energy

  • Intermediate in advanced biofuel production from sugars and cellulose
  • Precursor to energy-dense blending components improving gasoline quality
  • Supports renewable diesel- and jet-fuel pathways
