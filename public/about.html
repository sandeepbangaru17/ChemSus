About ChemSus Technologies

Innovating at the intersection of chemistry and sustainability to provide high-purity chemical solutions.

ChemSus Technologies Private Limited is an Indian specialty chemicals company dedicated to the manufacturing of value-added products. Our operations are guided by the principles of green chemistry, aiming for safer, cleaner, and more efficient industrial processes.

Who We Are

Incorporated on 29 May 2023 and registered in Assam, India, ChemSus Technologies operates from North Guwahati. We have strong roots in the region, including incubation support from ecosystems like IIT Guwahati, allowing us to serve customers across the nation and globally.
