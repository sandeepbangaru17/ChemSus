RENEWABLE BIOMASS TO CHEMISTRY

Lignocellulose-based Specialty Chemicals

Sustainable, green and future-ready chemical solutions derived from renewable biomass for health, food and industrial applications.

Objective

Our mission

To develop sustainable, green and futuristic chemical processes using renewable lignocellulosic biomass.

ChemSus Technologies focuses on the development of specialty chemicals including cosmetic and food preservatives, disinfectants, active pharmaceutical ingredients, anti-corrosion agents, pesticides and ingredients for paints, inks and polymers.

Our processes emphasize eco-friendly chemistry, scalability and reduced dependence on fossil-based resources.

Green process development Biomass-derived intermediates Reduced fossil dependence
