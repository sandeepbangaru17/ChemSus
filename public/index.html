Lignocellulose-based Specialty Chemicals

Sustainable and green specialty chemicals and pharmaceuticals derived from renewable lignocellulosic biomass for health, food and industrial applications.

Objective

Our mission

To develop sustainable, green and futuristic chemical processes using renewable lignocellulosic biomass.

ChemSus Technologies focuses on the development of specialty chemicals including cosmetic and food preservatives, disinfectants, active pharmaceutical ingredients, anti-corrosion agents, pesticides and ingredients for paints, inks and polymers.

Our processes emphasize eco-friendly chemistry, scalability and reduced dependence on fossil-based resources.

Green process development Biomass-derived intermediates Reduced fossil dependence

What We Focus On

Core areas

Sustainable Chemistry

Green processes built on renewable lignocellulosic biomass, designed for lower carbon footprint and circular use of resources.

Specialty Chemicals

High-value products for pharmaceutical, food, cosmetic and industrial applications, with strong emphasis on performance and safety.

Industrial Scale-up

End-to-end development from laboratory research through pilot-scale validation to commercial deployment with partners.
